rd /s /q "C:\path\to\your\directory"

rd : remove directory
/s : recursively (dig into the directory and all sub-directories till the end)
/q : quietly, don't prompt for confirmation

del /s /q .\*
deletes all files recursively.
does not delete folders

for /d %%D in (*) do rd /s /q "%%D"
loops over all directories (for /d) and removes them (rd /s /q removes the folder and everything under it without asking).
